# Complete List of Creatures in Spells 'n Squares

This document lists all creature types registered in the mod, organized by category.

**Source:** `src/main/java/at/koopro/spells_n_squares/features/creatures/ModCreatures.java`

---

## Companion Creatures (17)

Tamable companion creatures that can be kept as pets:

1. **Owl** - A magical owl that can deliver mail and items
2. **Cat** - A familiar cat companion with loyalty mechanics
3. **Toad** - A pet toad companion
4. **Niffler** - A treasure-hunting creature that finds valuable items
5. **Bowtruckle** - A small tree guardian that helps with plant growth
6. **Puffskein** - A fluffy pet creature that provides comfort and regeneration
7. **Kneazle** - An intelligent cat-like creature that can detect untrustworthy people
8. **Erumpent** - An explosive horned creature, dangerous but tamable
9. **Mooncalf** - A shy creature that appears during full moon
10. **Raven Familiar** - An intelligent bird familiar companion
11. **Rat Familiar** - A small and quick familiar companion
12. **Snake Familiar** - A snake familiar companion
13. **Ferret Familiar** - A playful and quick familiar companion
14. **Augurey** - Also known as the Irish Phoenix, a greenish-black bird whose cry was once believed to foretell death
15. **Demiguise** - A peaceful, ape-like creature that can turn invisible and has precognitive abilities
16. **Fwooper** - A brightly colored bird whose song can drive listeners insane
17. **Jobberknoll** - A small, blue speckled bird that remains silent until its death

## Neutral Creatures (18)

Non-hostile creatures that can be tamed:

1. **Billywig** - A sapphire-blue insect native to Australia; its sting causes giddiness and levitation
2. **Centaur** - A creature with the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse
3. **Clabbert** - A tree-dwelling creature resembling a cross between a monkey and a frog
4. **Diricawl** - A plump, flightless bird capable of vanishing and reappearing elsewhere
5. **Fairy** - A small, humanoid creature with insect-like wings
6. **Fire Crab** - A large, turtle-like creature that shoots flames from its rear end
7. **Ghoul** - A slimy, buck-toothed creature that often inhabits attics and barns
8. **Gnome** - A small, mischievous creature that infests gardens
9. **Horklump** - A pink, bristly creature that resembles a mushroom
10. **Imp** - A small, mischievous creature similar to a pixie but less dangerous
11. **Jarvey** - A ferret-like creature capable of human speech, though it usually speaks in rude and fast phrases
12. **Leprechaun** - A small, mischievous creature known for its love of gold
13. **Re'em** - A giant oxen with golden hides, whose blood grants immense strength
14. **Sphinx** - A creature with the head of a human and the body of a lion, known for posing riddles
15. **Streeler** - A giant snail that changes color hourly and leaves a poisonous trail
16. **Troll** - A large, dim-witted creature known for its immense strength
17. **Unicorn** - A pure-white, horse-like creature with a single horn on its forehead
18. **Yeti** - Also known as the Abominable Snowman, a giant humanoid creature native to the Himalayas

## Mount Creatures (6)

Tamable creatures that can be ridden:

1. **Hippogriff** - A mountable flying creature
2. **Thestral** - A mountable flying creature visible only to those who've seen death
3. **Occamy** - A shape-shifting serpent that can grow/shrink, protective of eggs
4. **Thunderbird** - A large bird that creates storms, powerful flying mount
5. **Graphorn** - A large aggressive beast, very tough and powerful mount
6. **Zouwu** - A fast cat-like mount that can teleport short distances

## Hostile Creatures (28)

Dangerous creatures that attack players:

1. **Dementor** - A soul-sucking entity that requires a Patronus to defeat
2. **Boggart** - A shape-shifting fear entity
3. **Acromantula** - A large spider enemy
4. **Dragon** - A rare, powerful boss creature (generic)
5. **Swooping Evil** - A venomous flying creature for combat
6. **Basilisk** - A giant serpent with petrifying gaze, extremely dangerous boss
7. **Chimaera** - A multi-headed beast, dangerous hybrid creature
8. **Ashwinder** - A serpent that arises from magical fires left burning too long
9. **Doxy** - A small, fairy-like creature with venomous bites
10. **Erkling** - An elf-like creature that lures children with music before eating them
11. **Lethifold** - A dangerous, black, cloak-like creature that suffocates its victims in their sleep
12. **Manticore** - A beast with the head of a man, body of a lion, and tail of a scorpion
13. **Nundu** - A giant leopard-like creature whose breath causes disease and death
14. **Pixie** - A small, mischievous creature known for its pranks
15. **Quintaped** - A five-legged creature with a taste for human flesh
16. **Red Cap** - A dwarf-like creature that lurks in places where blood has been shed

## Aquatic Creatures (6)

Water-dwelling creatures:

1. **Hippocampus** - A sea creature with the head and front legs of a horse and the tail of a fish
2. **Kappa** - A water-dwelling creature from Japan that resembles a monkey with fish-like scales
3. **Kelpie** - A shape-shifting water demon that often appears as a horse
4. **Merpeople** - Aquatic beings with the upper body of a human and the tail of a fish
5. **Ramora** - A silver fish native to the Indian Ocean, known for its power to anchor ships
6. **Grindylow** - A pale-green water demon with long, thin fingers and sharp horns
